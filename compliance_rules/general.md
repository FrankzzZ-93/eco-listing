# Amazon Listing Compliance Rules — General

## Title Rules
- Maximum 200 characters
- No ALL CAPS (except brand names and standard acronyms like USB, LED)
- No promotional phrases (e.g., "Sale", "Free Shipping", "Best Seller")
- No special characters for decoration (e.g., ★, ♥, →)
- Must start with brand name or primary keyword

## Bullet Point Rules
- Maximum 5 bullet points
- Each bullet point maximum 500 characters
- No pricing information
- No promotional language
- No competitor references by name
- Start each bullet with a capital letter

## Description Rules
- No HTML tags (plain text or A+ Content only)
- No external links or URLs
- No contact information
- No warranty or guarantee language that cannot be substantiated

## Forbidden Words and Phrases
The following words/phrases are PROHIBITED in any part of the listing:
- "best"
- "cheapest"
- "#1"
- "guaranteed"
- "number one"
- "top rated"
- "best seller"
- "free" (unless describing a genuine free component)
- "bonus"
- "limited time"
- "act now"
- "don't miss"
- "exclusive deal"

## Content Restrictions
- No subjective claims without qualification (e.g., say "designed for durability" instead of "the most durable")
- No time-sensitive language
- No pricing or discounts
- No references to Amazon rankings or reviews
- No requests for reviews or ratings
